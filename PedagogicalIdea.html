Our pedagogical idea

We make use of two digital libraries with millions of books to choose from. Our Book Recommender for Schools helps you to make the best use of these two vast libraries. We have carefully made a selection of books suitable for pupils in different grade levels. We have organised and classified these books according to grade levels and different subjects. Your pupil only has to answer a few questions in the questionnaire on our website and our Book Recommender will give them a personalised recommendation for books to read which are both appropriate for their age and answering to their personal interests and motivations. With Book Recommender for Schools, the books we recommend are always free and available for instantaeous download for students, both for their school laptops or their personal devices. The easy downloading of the books allows your students easily to sample the books offered and in case the recommendation was not for their taste, the questionnaire can be used to give a new one.
